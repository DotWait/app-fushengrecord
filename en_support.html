Frequently Asked Questions

  • Will my data be lost?

All data is stored locally on your device. If the app is deleted or the device is replaced or damaged, your data may not be recoverable. We recommend backing up your device regularly.

  • How do I enable Face ID or Touch ID protection?

You can enable privacy protection in the app’s Settings page.

  • Why is location access unavailable?

Please go to: iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services, then enable permissions for Moment Journal.

  • How can I report bugs or submit feedback?

Feel free to contact us by email.
