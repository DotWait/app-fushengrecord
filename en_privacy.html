Moment Journal Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 7, 2026

Welcome to Moment Journal. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information and data security. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully before using the app.

1. Information We Collect

To provide core app functionality, Moment Journal may collect:

  • Text, photos, videos, and audio content you create
  • Location information you choose to save
  • Basic device information for app stability and crash diagnostics

We do not collect unnecessary personal information unrelated to the app’s functionality.

2. Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • The app does not provide cloud synchronization
  • We cannot access, read, analyze, or sell your journal content
  • Deleting the app or device damage may result in permanent data loss

3. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Photos: to select and save images or videos
  • Camera: to capture photos and videos
  • Microphone: to record audio
  • Location: to save places and memories
  • Notifications: to remind you to journal

All permissions require your explicit authorization and can be disabled anytime in iOS Settings.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your local data, including:

  • Local data protection
  • Face ID / Touch ID lock
  • System-level secure storage

5. Third-Party Services

The app may use Apple system services such as Photos, Notifications, and Location Services.

Except for required system integrations, your journal content is not uploaded to third-party servers.

6. Children’s Privacy

If you are under the age of majority in your region, please use the app under parental or guardian supervision.

7. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time as the app evolves. Updated versions will be published on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the support page.
